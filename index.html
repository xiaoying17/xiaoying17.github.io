design theories and principles

发表于 2018-05-23

three aspects to graphic design

1) layout
2) color
3) typography

things to keep in mind

1) konw your audience and the goal of the piece
2) a designer must be a great communicator
3) order and clarity make information easy to understand
4) build intuition through practice

key rules and concepts of design

1 balance : symmetrical asymmetrical
2 proximity : the grouping and shaping of objects on a page
3 alignment : keeping objects in-line with one another
4 contrast : creating distinction by highlighting differences
5 white space : the art of nothing
6 keep it simple : avoid an overwhelming amount of colors or objects
always ask yourself: what am I representing? how am I representing it?
winnow away superfluous information and details

digital workflows

software suites
1 Adobe Creative Suite is the industry standard for design
2 Microsoft Powerpoint can be used for simple presentation and print layouts

adobe indesign/ Adobe illustrator/ photoshop / powerpoint

color handing

CMYK is generally used for print, RGB is for on-screen media

types of digital artwork

1 vector
1)image consisting of points and paths that have a proportional relationship
2)infinitely scalable
3)primarily used in illustrator
2 raster(PNG)
1) flattened image consisting of many pixels
2) not scalable
3) primary used in photoshop, any print program

image resolution

270 dpi for print, 72 dpi for screen
the possible size of the final image will be dictated by the number of pixels in the image

color formula

1 Monochromatic use hue and saturation
2 Analogous use color next to it on the wheel
3 Complementory add some variety by introducing lighter, darker, desaturated tones
4 splite complementory use the colors on eaxh side of the complement
5 Triatic pretty striking especially with primary or secondary colors
6 Tetratic forms a rectangle on the wheel, using two complementory schemes, let one color dominate and the others serve as an accent

Windows users are not happy #629

发表于 2018-05-17

在windows环境下用node-gyp作为一个依赖来初始化项目永远是一个痛点。本次利用vue框架，其中用到sqlite3依赖包，一直报错，gyp err… can’t find python等。根据出错提示，明白需要在win10环境下安装python,并设置其环境变量，还需要安装Visual C++的依赖环境。网上众说纷纭，总结教训，及时查看文档，github上有关于安装node-gyp的详细说明。

参考https://github.com/nodejs/node-gyp,在windows系统上安装node-gyp有以下两种方法。
其一是通过npm自己提供的依赖包来配置系统所需的工具和配置，具体命令为npm install –global –production windows-build-tools,以管理员身份在命令行运行。体会到一点，由于这些依赖包也是与时俱进一直在变化的，不是说这个命令就能完全解决问题，存在版本的更迭和一系列发现issue和完善bug的过程。
其二是手动添加依赖环境。第一步，安装visual C++构建环境，笔者选择options1，安装默认的Visual C++ Build Tools(http://landinghub.visualstudio.com/visual-cpp-build-tools )。第二步，安装Python2.7版本同时设置系统环境变量。第三步，nom config set msvs_version 2015。另外，如果安装了多个Python版本，需指明一个版本。

其他收获: Windows系统上Node.js环境配置，以上过程即为设置的过程。而sqlite3包恰好可以用来检验这个环境是否配置正确。此外，还可以安装node-sass等来检验。当然，不需要时，再通过npm uninstall卸载。

Elements that you should animate(UX)

Part 1 Transitions

Performant Properties: position scale rotation opacity,不会引起重流重绘。
Triggering: 1、Hover pseudo class 2、Class changes

Part 2 Keyframes

Keyframes is what animation does, and the animation is how it’s done,like speed ,time.

Triggering: 1、Hover pseduo class 2、Class changes 3、new elements
注意transform是个属性，可以scale放大缩小、rotate旋转等，动画是从一个状态到另一个状态。

When to Animate

1.Orientation orient people to where they are from where they were,用户从一个界面到另一个界面，两个界面用这个动画连接。
2.Functional Change answers what does this element do now。eg:button有on、off状态，可以清楚地显示现在是在哪个状态。
3.New Element help people know something new happened here.
4.Highlight little pulsing bring our attention to that spot
5.Visual Feedback eg:mac系统邮件点击发送后，提交界面向上滑动，有个模拟发送的效果。make the layout understand what I am asking it to do. animation represent that idea with motion.

twitter点赞效果

发表于 2018-04-12

今天在codepen上看到一个推特点赞的动画，来学习一下。材料需要一张雪碧图，链接在这里。图有29帧。这个动画通过设置transition来改变background-position背景图像的起始位置来实现。
当用户点赞时，background-position的值以steps函数阶段性的过渡效果从0变到-2800px，实现动画效果。当用户取消点赞时，background-position位置从-2800px到0，同时加上默认的transition-duration: 0s;达到没有动画的从红心到白心的回退效果。

对于transition,通过transition-property属性设置过渡效果的css属性名称，以transition-duration规定过渡效果需要的时间定为1s,以transition-timing-function规定速度效果的速度曲线。

深克隆与浅克隆

发表于 2018-04-01

区别

深克隆与浅克隆针对的是js中的复杂数据类型(Object,Array等)。对象的实例是存储在堆内存中然后通过一个引用值去操作对象，因此有复制引用和复制实例两种情况，极为浅克隆与深克隆的区别。
浅克隆是复制引用，复制后的引用都是指向同一个对象的实例，彼此间的操作会互相影响。而深克隆是在堆中重新分配内存，把源对象实例的所有属性都进行新建复制，复制后的对象与原来的对象是完全隔离的。
