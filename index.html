three aspects to graphic design

1) layout
2) color
3) typography

things to keep in mind

1) konw your audience and the goal of the piece
2) a designer must be a great communicator
3) order and clarity make information easy to understand
4) build intuition through practice

key rules and concepts of design

1 balance : symmetrical asymmetrical
2 proximity : the grouping and shaping of objects on a page
3 alignment : keeping objects in-line with one another
4 contrast : creating distinction by highlighting differences
5 white space : the art of nothing
6 keep it simple : avoid an overwhelming amount of colors or objects
always ask yourself: what am I representing? how am I representing it?
winnow away superfluous information and details

2)infinitely scalable
3)primarily used in illustrator
2 raster(PNG)
1) flattened image consisting of many pixels
2) not scalable
3) primary used in photoshop, any print program

color formula

1 Monochromatic use hue and saturation
